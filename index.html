Grand Theft Auto : V (GTA 5)
Grand Theft Auto V is an action-adventure game set in the vast open world of Los Santos, where players can control three different characters as they navigate a story filled with heists, crime, and chaos. The game features both single-player and online multiplayer modes, offering endless exploration, missions, and activities in a dynamic city.
Learn more about GTA 5
Red Dead Redemption : 2 (RDR 2)
Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world action-adventure game set in the late 1800s during the decline of the American frontier. Players follow the story of Arthur Morgan, an outlaw and member of the Van der Linde gang, as he grapples with loyalty and survival in a changing world. The game offers a rich narrative, expansive landscapes, and a deeply immersive experience.
Learn more about RDR 2
God of War : RAGNARÖK (GoW)
God of War: RAGNARÖK continues the journey of Kratos and his son Atreus as they face the apocalyptic events of Norse mythology. With a focus on father-son dynamics, epic battles, and stunning visuals, the game features a deeply emotional narrative intertwined with Norse gods, monsters, and the unfolding of RAGNARÖK, the end of days.
Learn more about God of War: RAGNARÖK
Uncharted : 4 - A Thief's End (UC 4)
Uncharted 4: A Thief's End is an action-adventure game following treasure hunter Nathan Drake as he embarks on one final quest. Set across stunning global locations, the game combines exploration, puzzle-solving, and cinematic storytelling as players search for a legendary pirate treasure, while uncovering deep personal conflicts.
Learn more about Uncharted 4: A Thief's End
Ghost of Tsushima (GoT)
Ghost of Tsushima is an open-world action game set in 13th-century feudal Japan. Players control Jin Sakai, a samurai warrior fighting to protect his homeland from the Mongol invasion. The game offers an immersive experience with stunning landscapes, swordplay combat, and stealth mechanics, blending history with the legend of the Ghost.
Learn more about Ghost of Tsushima
Assassins Creed : MIRAGE
Assassin's Creed Mirage brings the series back to its stealth roots, set in a beautifully detailed 9th-century Baghdad. Players control Basim, using parkour, pickpocketing, and creative assassinations to complete missions. Black Box missions offer multiple approaches, demanding patience and strategy. Combat is minimal, pushing players to avoid direct confrontations. With about 20 hours of gameplay, it provides a compact but immersive experience.
Learn more about Assassins Creed: MIRAGE
